Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredient Amount
Spaghetti 500g
Pancetta 250g
Olive oil 1 tablespoon
Eggs 1 whole egg, 4 egg yolks
Pecorino cheese 50g
Parmesan 50g
Sea salt 1 tablespoon
Pepper 20g
  1. In a pot of boiling salted water, add the pasta and cook as per packet directions.
  2. Heat a pan on low heat. Add oil and pancetta and cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until pancetta starts to brown.
  3. Place egg and egg yolks, pecorino, parmesan in a bowl and whisk together, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add pasta and Â½ cup of pasta water into the pan and toss to coat in the oil.
  5. Remove pan from heat and add egg and cheese mixture into the pasta immediately.
  6. Toss rigorously to cook the egg evenly in the hot pasta, but not scramble.
  7. If it starts to curdle, add a little more pasta water.
  8. To finish top with a generous amount of cracked pepper over the top.
  9. Serve and enjoy with family and friends!
